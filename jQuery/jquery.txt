CDNs?
=> CDN (Content Delivery Network): A CDN is a network of distributed servers that work together to deliver web content,
   like images, stylesheets, and scripts, to users based on their geographical location. CDNs aim to reduce latency and 
   enhance website performance by serving content from servers closer to the user, resulting in faster load times and improved user experience. 
   Popular CDN providers include Cloudflare, Akamai, and Amazon CloudFront.
